Test environment rules
1. No other person is allowed to enter the room while taking the exam.
2. The lighting in the room must be bright .Overhead lighting is preferred.
3. You must sit at a clean desk or table.
4. The desk or walls around you must not have any writing.
5. The room must be quiet. Sounds such as music/television are not permitted.
6. The following items must not be on your desk or used during your proctored exam :
• Books
• Paper
• Pens
• Calculators
• Textbooks
• Notebooks
• Phones
